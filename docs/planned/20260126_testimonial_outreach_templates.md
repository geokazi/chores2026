## Targeting Criteria

### Who to Ask (High Response Rate)

| Signal | Why |
|--------|-----|
| 50+ chores completed | Proven engagement |
| 3+ weekly goals hit | Success = happy user |
| Active 14-30 days | Past initial churn window |
| Multiple kids using app | Family buy-in |
| Logged in 5+ days/week | Power user |

### Who NOT to Ask

| Signal | Why |
|--------|-----|
| < 7 days active | Too early, feels pushy |
| Recent support ticket | May have unresolved issues |
| Declining engagement | Churn risk, bad timing |
| Already asked in 60 days | Avoid fatigue |
